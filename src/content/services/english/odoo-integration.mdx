## Odoo Integration Services

Your ERP doesn't exist in isolation. NETLINKS ensures Odoo works seamlessly with every system in your technology stack — from payment gateways and eCommerce platforms to shipping providers, CRM tools, and custom APIs.

### Integration Capabilities

**eCommerce Integrations**
Connect Odoo with Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and custom eCommerce platforms for real-time inventory sync, order management, and unified customer data.

**Payment Gateway Connectors**
Integrate with Stripe, PayPal, Square, and regional payment providers to automate payment processing and reconciliation.

**Shipping & Logistics**
Connect with FedEx, UPS, DHL, and local carriers for automated shipping label generation, tracking, and delivery management.

**Accounting & Banking**
Sync Odoo with banking systems, QuickBooks, and financial platforms for automated bank reconciliation and financial reporting.

**CRM & Marketing**
Integrate with HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, and other marketing tools to maintain a single source of truth for customer data.

**Custom API Development**
Build custom REST and XML-RPC integrations to connect Odoo with proprietary systems, legacy applications, and industry-specific software.

### Our Integration Approach

1. **System Audit** — We map your current technology landscape
2. **Architecture Design** — Plan the integration architecture for reliability and scale
3. **Development & Testing** — Build and rigorously test all integration points
4. **Deployment & Monitoring** — Go live with real-time monitoring and alerting
5. **Ongoing Maintenance** — Keep integrations running smoothly as systems evolve
